Data Sovereignty CRITICAL

All data resides exclusively within your infrastructure. VitaNova servers are never involved in data transit or storage. Deploy on-premises, on your cloud instance, or in a government-certified environment.

Air-Gap Compatibility TACTICAL

Full offline operation with local intelligence processing. Data syncs when connectivity is restored. Designed for restricted environments where external network access is prohibited.

Anonymization Pipeline PRIVACY

Before specialist access: real identity → persistent pseudonym, absolute timestamps → relative offsets, geographic data → stripped. GDPR and medical data regulation compliant by architecture.

Smart Batching Protocol EFFICIENCY

WorkManager-orchestrated upload cadence: critical vitals every 15min, aggregated scores hourly, raw sensor data on charge+WiFi. Under 2% battery overhead in all tested configurations.
